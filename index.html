about The OM Studio

Oti & Marius Dance Studio was opened in September 2020, located in North-West London. Founded by Oti Mabuse & Marius Iepure, the duo has created a dynamic and raw dance studio to inspire and host the next generation of dancers. With a passion for all things dance, Oti’s dream is to lead a beautiful dance school where she can share her love for dancing.!

The studio itself has been host to some internationally renowned dance workshops, events and competitions. Alongside this, we have seen our own dance courses and classes grow and produce some truly remarkable dancers. With a predominant focus on Ballroom and Latin American dance, we have something for everyone at OMDS. With courses ranging from beginners to intermediate, private lessons, wedding lessons and exclusive ProAm camps.

OMDS is proud to have such an inclusive dance studio that values the true joy that dance brings!
