OM Proam

Have you ever watched Strictly Come Dancing and thought, “I could do that?”. Well here, you can!

At OMDS, we offer a Pro Am dance package that pairs you with a dance professional to become your mentor and coach. Within these packages, your dance partner will become your team! You will learn together the beauty of Ballroom and Latin American dance and be Quickstepping your way into show dances and competitions in no time.

Find the joy in dance with our Pro Am experiences whilst we take an educational approach to developing your dance technique one step at a time. As a Pro Am dancer, you and your dance partner will have exclusive opportunities to perform at OMDS events and external events in association with Oti Mabuse and Marius Iepure.

What are you waiting for?! Let us guide you under the glitterball today!
