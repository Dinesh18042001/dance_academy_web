Summary for OM Schools

Instructor of dance school

We love to bring the wonderful world of dance to as many people in our local community as possible, and what better way to do this than to start with our work in schools!

Most of us have seen the research on physical and mental health benefits of exercise in schools, but we believe dance is a fun, energetic way to tick all these boxes. The benefits dance has on children and within education are extraordinary. When we think about the ways dance encourages our coordination, musicality, confidence and social skills, it really makes perfect sense to bring this into educational frameworks and deliver these skills to children of all ages and backgrounds!

We offer a variety of dance programmes within schools, including:

. In Curriculum Dance
. After School and Before School Sessions
. Dance Exams
